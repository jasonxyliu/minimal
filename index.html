Hello and Welcome!

I am a computer science Ph.D. student at Brown University. I am fortunately to be advised by Prof. Stefanie Tellex and to work with Prof. George Konidaris. My research is supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship and Cooke Graduate Scholarship.

Research Interests

I am interested in building intelligent agents that can perform long-horizon tasks in diverse environments provided with sparse reward signals. To that end, I would like to leverage structures in natural language commands from humans and vision to help machines plan and learn more correctly and efficiently.
